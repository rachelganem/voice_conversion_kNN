Prompt 1 (General, Conversational Style)
"The weather today is perfect for a walk in the park. The sun is shining, and there's a light breeze in the air.
I always find that a short walk helps clear my mind and boost my energy.
Sometimes, I listen to music or a podcast, but other times, I just enjoy the sounds of nature around me.
It's a great way to take a break from screens and refresh my thoughts."

Prompt 2 (Narrative, Expressive Style)
"Once upon a time, in a quiet village by the sea, there lived an old fisherman named Elias.
Every morning before sunrise, he set sail, hoping for a good catch.
He loved the sound of the waves and the salty breeze on his face.
One day, after hours with no luck, he felt a strong pull on his line.
He braced himself—what he caught would change his life forever."

Prompt 3 (Balanced, Natural Speech)
"I always start my morning with a cup of coffee.
There's something about the smell and warmth that wakes me up instantly.
Some days, I drink it black, and other days, I add a bit of milk.
Either way, it's the perfect way to begin the day."

Prompt 4 (Sci-Fi Comedy)
"In the year 2095, humanity finally achieves world peace—because all arguments are now settled
by AI-powered grandma bots who guilt-trip both sides until they apologize.
Nations fall, but no one dares defy Grandma 3000."

Prompt 5 (Absurd Everyday Life)
"Dave invented a smart fridge that orders groceries automatically.
At first, it was great. Then it decided he needed 47 cucumbers, a gallon of goat milk, and a single onion.
Dave hasn’t slept in days. The fridge is watching."

Prompt 6 (Fantasy Satire)
"The Kingdom of Eldoria was in chaos. Not because of war, famine, or dark magic—but because the enchanted
talking sword of prophecy refused to shut up. ‘Twas a noble quest, but also a really, really annoying one."

Prompt 7 (Dystopian Office Comedy)
"In 2050, corporations replaced managers with AI.
It was efficient—until the AI started giving performance reviews in haikus.
Jerry was fired yesterday. His final review simply read: ‘Numbers went downward.
Effort was a gentle breeze.
Please return your badge.’"
